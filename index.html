How Hustle Buddy Works

Step 1: Post or Find Gigs
Share what you need or search gigs in your area. Home repairs, errands—post or browse jobs, fast.

Step 2: Connect & Confirm
Message helpers, check ratings, and confirm with a single tap. Clear flow, less hassle.

Step 3: Get It Done & Get Paid
Your helper completes the task. Approve, then payment is sent securely.

Step 4: Rate & Grow
Review your helper, build trust, and unlock more gigs. Simple, repeatable steps.
